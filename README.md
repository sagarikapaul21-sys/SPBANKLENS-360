SPBANKLENS 360
Enterprise Banking Operations & Risk Intelligence Platform (Prototype)
Conceptualized & Developed by Sagarika Paul

An AI-assisted prototype that consolidates fragmented banking operational information into a single role-based operational intelligence platform for Branch, Regional Office, and Central Office users.

The Problem
In branch banking, especially in high-volume and rural operations, operational information is often scattered across:

Multiple Excel trackers
Physical loan files
Email communications
Inspection registers
Recovery follow-up sheets
Legal case registers
CGTMSE tracking sheets
Individual officers' notebooks
Finding a single document may require searching through 100–200 pages of loan files, while critical follow-up dates can be missed due to lean staffing and fragmented information.

SPBANKLENS 360 was conceptualized to explore how these operational activities could be consolidated into a single read-only operational intelligence layer without replacing the Core Banking System.

Solution
SPBANKLENS 360 provides role-based operational visibility through dedicated dashboards for:

Branch
Regional Office
Central Office
The platform supports portfolio monitoring, operational governance, workflow management, and account-level visibility while preserving operational continuity.
