# SPBANKLENS 360

Enterprise Banking Operations & Risk Intelligence Platform

Conceptualized & Developed by Sagarika Paul

## Overview

SPBANKLENS 360 is a prototype banking operational intelligence platform designed to improve operational visibility across Branches, Regional Offices and Central Office.

## Key Features

- Branch Dashboard
- Regional Dashboard
- Central Office Dashboard
- Account 360
- Knowledge Notebook
- Rule-Based Risk Engine
- Early Warning System
- Workflow Management
- Recovery Management
- Legal Management
- CGTMSE Management
- Inspection Dashboard
- Executive War Room
